Fill jury decision

This bookmarklet pastes a sailing jury decision from your Google Doc into the right form, in one click. It automatically detects whether the page is sailti, RRS (racingrulesofsailing.org), or m2s (manage2sail) and fills the matching fields.

1. Install

Drag the button below onto your bookmarks bar:

Fill jury decision

↑ drag me up to the bookmarks bar

Bookmarks bar hidden? Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows / Linux) or ⌘+Shift+B (macOS) to show it.

2. Use

  1. In the Google Doc, click the red flag icon on the right edge, then Open jury panel.
  2. In the panel, click Copy for sailti / RRS / m2s, then Copy to clipboard.
  3. Open the decision form — sailti, RRS, or m2s.
  4. Click the Fill jury decision bookmark.

On RRS and m2s the bookmarklet switches to the Hearing tab automatically before filling. Validity checks, witnesses, scoring, and other system-specific fields stay manual.

Updates

The bookmark loads the latest script from this site on every click — no need to reinstall when the script changes.
