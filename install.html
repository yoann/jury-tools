Fill jury decision

This bookmarklet pastes a sailing jury decision from your Google Doc into the website's decision form, in one click.

1. Install

Drag the button below onto your bookmarks bar:

Fill jury decision

↑ drag me up to the bookmarks bar

Bookmarks bar hidden? Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows / Linux) or ⌘+Shift+B (macOS) to show it.

2. Use

  1. In the Google Doc, click Jury → Copy decision for website, then Copy to clipboard.
  2. Open the website's decision form.
  3. Click the Fill jury decision bookmark.

Updates

The bookmark loads the latest script from this site on every click — no need to reinstall when the script changes.
